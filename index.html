the paper contest

Students compete against other students in their students chapter for the opportunity to advance to the national Student Paper Contest held during STECON and win chapter PRIZES.

Contestants enter an abstract of their paper and present it on the day of the submission. The papers of the winners who proceed to the national Student Paper Contest at STECON will be published on IEEEPES FUTO STUDENT's CHAPTER social media handles and website.

Important Deadlines & Details

  • Paper Submission Opens : 22 April 2022

    Once registration are completed you can submit your abstract in doc format to (gregoryamaefule@ieee.org) be reviewed by our judges. Only single-authored papers are allowed for this contest and they must be based on original work. Abstracts can be a maximum of 425 words.

  • Paper Submission Ends : 30 April 2022

    If you have missed your deadline, you cannot participate and you will need to wait until next year to submit.

  • Online Contest Begins : 1 May 2022

    After the close of paper submission, abstract of participants will be posted on our social media pages. All participants are expected to follow us on all handles and encourage your friends to do so. The number of engagements on your article will form 30% of your final score.

  • Online Contest Ends : 5 May 2022

    Participate now will be graded based on the number of engagements on their abstract. There would be a prize for highest engagements from this category separate from the main prize.

  • Judges Announce Winners : 9 May 2022

    Winners of the contest would be notified and posted on our social media handles

  • Winners Receive Prize and are Published : 10 May 2022

    Winners would recieve their respective prizes and their abstract published
